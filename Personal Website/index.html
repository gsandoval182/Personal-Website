Overview

Welcome to my personal site! The goal of this site is to provide the reader with information about myself, my career goals, as well as my work and educational experiences. I plan to incorporate a captivating design, images, and engage the reader but keep a very business professional look to the overall design. 
My name is Greg Sandoval and I am a student at the University of New Mexico. I have two more years until I complete my Bachelor’s degree where I plan to use my major in Communication on a career in sports and media. I hope to focus on all types of sports - from the digital world to the physical, I am a fan of watching competitive matches playout across any stage. In this career I would like to bring marketing and communication business solutions to teams and athletes across the nation.
My passion has always been in sports, and later in the design and development of content. As I grow my education in Communication, I hope to provide more work and experience in copy writing and editing. I have experience in creating marketing assets and continue to take courses that allow me to develop my skills more. I hope to bring these two together to provide exceptional solutions to businesses.
In this site, you’ll be able to learn more about myself, design portfolio, and the work I’ve done. If you have any questions or would like to connect with me on any topic, please visit the Contact page.
